Refund & Rescheduling Policy

Please review our booking, refund, and rescheduling terms before confirming your class.

  • All class bookings must be confirmed before the scheduled training date.
  • Payments made for CPR classes are non-refundable unless SaintVal CPR & Safety cancels the class.
  • Students may request to reschedule their class with at least 24 hours’ notice.
  • Rescheduling requests made less than 24 hours before class may be subject to a rescheduling fee or forfeiture of payment.
  • No-shows without prior notice are non-refundable.
  • Group training deposits, if collected, are non-refundable but may be applied to one rescheduled date if proper notice is given.
  • SaintVal CPR & Safety reserves the right to reschedule classes due to emergencies, low enrollment, or unforeseen circumstances.
